Instruction to fill the Registeration form
Teachers are requested to kindly read all the instructions before filling the form.
  1. Fields marked with asterisk (*) sign are mandatory to fill in the form.
  2. Fill all your details correctly. You will not be allowed to edit the details once your submission of the application is done.
  3. Kindly enter your all-personal details as per your matriculation certificate.
  4. Address provided should be the address in which you are currently residing with all your necessary details.
  5. Scanned copy of a recent passport size photograph in JPEG/JPG/PNG format only. Photograph must be a recent (not older than 2 months) passport size colour photograph with light background and the size of file (jpeg/jpg/png format) should not be more than 3MB passport size photograph.
  6. A valid email account which has not been used for filling the form by any other applicant applying for the registration. Two or more applicants cannot share the same email ID. All future correspondence will be made through the registered email ID only.
  7. Verify your email using verify email button before processing further in the application.
  8. Enter your school’s name, school code and school-ID scanned photograph provided you by the school management which should only be in JPEG/JPG/PNG format and not more the 3MB.
  9. Enter your specialization subject. (only one)
  10. Board of the school with which school is registered.
  11. Pick your class you teach from the dropdown menu. Specify that on selection of class XI & XII you should pick the appropriate stream from the new dropdown menu below.
  12. Provide the details of the classes in which you teach. You can add more than one class in the form using the ADD MORE button.
  13. Select the subject’s checkbox which ever you teach in the school.
  14. Provide the specific subject codes for every subject in the given subject code input box which ever you have selected in the subject you teach checkbox.
  15. If you don’t find your subject in the given list kindly used CLICK HERE TO ADD IT button to add your subject and provide the specific code for the subject.
  16. While entering your password you should make sure that you fulfill all the parameters of the password field which will be shown as soon as you will enter your password in the password field, after fulfilling all the criteria confirm password input box will be displayed automatically.
  17. Make sure that your password should match with the confirm password, else your form will not be processed further.
  18. Enter the captcha code given in the Captcha box. You can refresh the captcha using the RE-CAPTCHA icon on the right side of the captcha image.
  19. Press the get registered button to submit your form.
  20. Progress of your form will be shown in the in-progress bar at the top of page.
  21. Kindly make sure that before submitting your form you have filled all your details correctly and all the mandatory fields of the form.
  22. All the major necessary details related to every field are given in the form as well.
  23. You can use RESET button to reset the form to the default settings of the form and all the filled details will be cleared automatically.
  24. Use the LOGIN button to login to your account if you are already a registered person.
  25. After successful submission of the form a Teacher ID will be generated and will be displayed on the screen , kindly note it down and the Teacher Id will also be sent to your registered emails for the future correspondence .
  26. For any “QUERY REGARDING THE REGISTRATION FORM”, the applicant may please contact to any of the provided contact no: +91-8433024414, +91-9368736361, +91-7409830173.
